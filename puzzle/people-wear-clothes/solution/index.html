Solvers are given 9 words and 9 crossword clues. The 9 words are arranged in the shapes of articles of clothing. Each word is a prefix for its category of clothing and describes a specific item in that category. A couple of examples are a CROP TOP or a BULLETPROOF VEST.

Each of the 9 crossword clues takes the shape of a person. Each person can “wear” one of the articles of clothing by overlapping a subset of the letters in the clothing item with letters in the crossword clue.

Combining the category of clothing with the answer to the crossword clue will yield a new word. Indexing into this word with the number in the clue gives the answer DRESS CODE.
