This puzzle contains some clues, followed by some images.

We can start out by identifying the images at the bottom of the puzzle. Each is a drawing of a container of some brand of beverage. The colors and shape of the containers are probably most useful here, but the sizes of the containers relative to each other are also approximately correct.

However, we still haven’t figured out what to do about the clues up top. The flavortext tells us that we “wanted drinks” but got something else instead, and we might also notice that the number of clues is equal to the number of drawings. Perhaps each one of the clues on top corresponds to one of the pictures at the bottom?

In fact, these clues are all a single letter substitution away from the name of a drink (listed in alphabetical order).
