This puzzle is presented as a water bottle with "5 free refills". The first thing we must do is identify the flavor of the water in the refill bottles. The flavors are, from 1 to 5: Crisp Apple, Strawberry Kiwi, Cherry, Lemon, and Blackberry.

Using the open eyes of the winking fruits as points, we draw lines along the water bottle from 1 to 2, 2 to 3, and so on:

Reading out the intersected letters, we get USE BRAND. We can use the shape of the bottle, or the list of 15 flavors on the main water bottle, to determine that the brand is Hint Water. Thus, we submit a hint request. The response we get is:

Hello,

Thank you for your hint request. From the information you've provided, we think the step you're missing is to remove the second, fifth and sixth letters, and to replace the last letter with T.

Sincerely,
ArnoldTheRhino
